How to play?

- Start game -

By clicking on Play!, the board will display itself and time counter will start counting time.

- Finish game -

Finish the game by matching all the animals on the board. Once you match all the animals, time will stop and you will see your score on Scoreboard!

- Score and Scoreboard -

Score is based on elapsed time and turn total!
Scoreboard will store your scores made during session and when you leave the page, it will dissapear.
