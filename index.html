How to play?

- Start game -

By clicking on Start!, the board will unlock and time counter will start counting time.

- Finish game -

Finish the game by matching all cards on the board. Once you match all the cards, time will stop and you will see your score!

- Score rating -

Score is based on elapsed time and flip total! So, be fast and don't flip unnecessary.
